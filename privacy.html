Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2, 2026

TL;DR: Flow Launcher does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any of your personal data. Everything happens locally on your device.

1. Data Collection & Analytics

Flow Launcher is built on the principle of absolute privacy. We do not use analytics SDKs, crash reporters, or any form of telemetry. We have zero knowledge of which apps you install, what music you listen to, or what events are on your calendar.

2. Why We Request Permissions

To function as a launcher and provide useful features, the app requires certain Android permissions. These are processed entirely on your device:

  • Query All Packages: Required to display the list of installed applications on your device so you can launch them.
  • Notification Listener Access: Optional. Required only if you enable the Media Player widget to read current track metadata and control playback.
  • Calendar Access: Optional. Required only if you enable the Calendar Agenda widget to display your upcoming events locally.

3. Internet Access

Flow Launcher does not require internet access to function. It does not communicate with any external servers or APIs.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on this page. However, our core philosophy of zero data collection will never change.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions regarding our Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.

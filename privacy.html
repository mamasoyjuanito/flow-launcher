TL;DR Flow Launcher is designed to operate locally on your device. We do not collect personal information for analytics, advertising, profiling or sale.

Data Collection & Analytics

Flow Launcher does not use analytics SDKs, crash reporting services, advertising SDKs or telemetry systems.

We do not collect information about installed applications, media playback, calendar events or launcher usage.

Local Storage

Flow Launcher stores settings, preferences, favorites and launcher configuration locally on your device in order to provide its functionality.

This information never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Permissions

  • QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES
    Required to display installed applications and allow you to launch them.
  • Notification Listener Access
    Optional. Used only for the Media Player widget to identify active media sessions and provide playback controls. Notification contents are not stored, transmitted or analyzed.
  • Calendar Access
    Optional. Used only to display upcoming events in the Agenda widget. Calendar information is processed entirely on-device.

Internet Access

Flow Launcher is designed to function without communicating with external servers or APIs.

Data Deletion

You can delete all Flow Launcher data at any time by:

  • Clearing application storage.
  • Uninstalling the application.

Because Flow Launcher does not use user accounts or cloud services, all data remains under your control.

Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy changes, the updated version will be published on this page together with a new revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you may open an issue on the GitHub repository or contact the project maintainer.

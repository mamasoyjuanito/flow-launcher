# Flow Launcher 🚀

Flow Launcher is a minimalist, elegant, and highly efficient Android launcher inspired by the Niagara Launcher philosophy. Built with Flutter, it focuses on providing a distraction-free experience while maintaining quick access to all your apps.

## ✨ Features

- **Minimalist Home:** Only see what you need—your clock and favorite apps.
- **Persistent App Cache:** Near-instant loading of all installed applications thanks to a persistent disk caching system.
- **Precision Alphabet Scroller:** A "Pixel-Perfect" sidebar for rapid navigation through your app list.
- **Glassmorphism Design:** A modern UI with deep blur effects, subtle borders, and transparent backgrounds that integrate seamlessly with your wallpaper.
- **Customizable Clock:** Choose from 10+ font styles (powered by Google Fonts) and adjust alignment (Left, Center, Right).
- **Adaptive Layout:** Ergonomic configuration to align the app list and scroller to either the left or right side of the screen.
- **Quick Actions:** Dedicated bottom corner buttons for Phone and Camera.
- **Intelligent Navigation:** Smart handling of system Home and Back gestures to always keep you focused on your favorites.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **Framework:** [Flutter](https://flutter.dev/) (v3.44.0)
- **State Management:** [Riverpod](https://riverpod.dev/)
- **Data Persistence:** [Shared Preferences](https://pub.dev/packages/shared_preferences)
- **App Management:** [Installed Apps](https://pub.dev/packages/installed_apps)
- **Typography:** [Google Fonts](https://pub.dev/packages/google_fonts)
- **Navigation:** [Scrollable Positioned List](https://pub.dev/packages/scrollable_positioned_list)
